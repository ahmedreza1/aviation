About VS Aviation & Hospitality

Let Your Dream Take A Flight

Since our inception over five decades ago, VS Aviation & Hospitality has been focused on building customised travel & tourism services for its clients.  Vs Aviation & Hospitality has diverse business interests including Airline Management  / GSA Representation, Destination Management Company, Cargo & Freight Forwarding, Chain of Restaurants and international Trading.

"With our roots starting in 1965"

VS Aviation & Hospitality aims to become one of the most prominent travel groups in India. VS Aviation & Hospitality has established a strong presence in the Indian market with a strategic focus to seek growth in the global business arena.
